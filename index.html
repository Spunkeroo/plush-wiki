History of Plush Toys

From the first Steiff teddy bear in 1902 to today's Squishmallow craze, plush toys have been beloved companions for over a century. The industry generates over $10 billion annually worldwide.

Major Plush Brands

Jellycat, Squishmallows, Build-A-Bear, Steiff, Gund, Aurora, and TY dominate the market. Each brand has distinct design philosophies, price points, and collector communities. Our wiki covers them all.

Collecting & Valuation

Rare plush toys can fetch thousands at auction. First-edition Beanie Babies, retired Jellycat designs, and limited Squishmallows are the most sought-after. Learn identification tips and fair market values.
